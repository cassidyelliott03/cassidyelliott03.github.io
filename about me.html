About Me

Hello my name is Cassidy Elliott and I am a graphic designer based in San Jose and Sacramento, California. I’m currently studying for a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design at San Jose State University. Since I was young, I have yearned for new knowledge and experiences. I take advantage of this quality by dedicating it to improving my craft and character. I find myself using bright and vibrant colors a lot because it sparks optimism and happiness, which describes my personality. Albeit my particular choice of art style, I am still open with creating designs outside of my aesthetic to satisfy my eagerness to explore and improve my skills.
